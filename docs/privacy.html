Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the SEO Dashboard for Google Sheets add-on ("the Add-on", "we", "us") handles your information when you use it.

1. Data We Access

The Add-on requests read-only access to the following Google services via OAuth 2.0:

  • Google Search Console — query performance data including keywords, clicks, impressions, CTR, and average position for your verified properties.
  • Google Analytics 4 — sessions, users, conversions, traffic channels, landing pages, and geographic data for your GA4 properties.
  • Google Sheets — to write report data into the spreadsheet where the Add-on is installed.

2. How We Use Your Data

All data accessed by the Add-on is used solely to populate the report tabs within your Google Spreadsheet. Specifically:

  • Search Console and Analytics data is fetched in real time when you run the report.
  • The data is written directly to your spreadsheet and nowhere else.
  • No data is transmitted to any external server operated by us or any third party.

3. Data Storage

The Add-on does not store your Search Console or Analytics data outside of your Google Spreadsheet. Your configuration settings (site URL, GA4 Property ID, date range) are stored in a hidden sheet within your spreadsheet and never leave your Google account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your data stays within your Google account at all times.

5. Google API Services

The Add-on's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Permissions

The Add-on requests only the minimum OAuth scopes necessary to function:

  • webmasters.readonly — read-only access to Search Console data
  • analytics.readonly — read-only access to Google Analytics data
  • spreadsheets — read/write access to the spreadsheet the Add-on is installed in
  • script.container.ui — display the configuration dialog within Sheets
  • script.scriptapp — create and manage scheduled triggers for automatic report refresh

7. Children's Privacy

The Add-on is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Add-on after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at randy.k.laird@gmail.com.
